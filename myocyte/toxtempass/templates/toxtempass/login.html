About ToxTempAssistant

ToxTempAssistant helps scientists document cell-based toxicological test methods more efficiently. Based on the ToxTemp template from OECD Guidance Document 211, the tool guides users through 77 structured questions.

Using large language models, ToxTempAssistant extracts and organises information from uploaded documents, shifting efforts from manual data entry to expert review, making transparent, standardised, and regulatory-ready documentation faster and easier.

Features
  • Seamless user login via email or ORCID, enabling secure access and integration with research profiles.
  • Export data in multiple formats, supporting reporting, sharing, and reproducibility in toxicological research.
  • Start by logging in or signing up to begin managing your assays.
